## Monthly Revenue
The analysis shows that there is a peak in sales in the second half of the year, leading up to the Christmas holidays.


## Top Countries by Revenue

The analysis shows that the United Kingdom generates the highest revenue, followed by several European countries such as Germany and France. 

This suggests that the business is heavily concentrated in specific geographic regions, with a strong dependence on the UK market.


## Top-Selling Products

The analysis identified the most frequently purchased products based on total quantity sold. 

Several decorative household items appeared among the top-selling products, suggesting strong customer demand for small giftable or decorative goods.

Removing non-product entries such as postage charges improved the clarity of product-level insights.


## Customer Behavior Analysis

Customer-level analysis revealed that a small group of customers generated a significant portion of total revenue. 

The distribution of customer revenue showed that most customers contributed smaller purchase amounts, while a smaller segment of high-value customers accounted for large purchases.

This pattern suggests opportunities for targeted marketing strategies focused on retaining high-value customers.
